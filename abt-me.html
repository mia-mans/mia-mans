MEET ME

Hi! I’m Mia, a third year undergraduate majoring in Physics and minoring in Design at the University of Pennsylvania. I was born and raised in Mānoa Valley of Honolulu, Hawaii and attended Punahou School through high school.

When I’m not studying the stars I can be found doing something creative. I’m a co-president of Soundworks Tap and publicist for Penn Dance Company.

In addition to dance, I enjoy sewing my own clothes and have begun learning front-end web design. My CSS and HTML skills are elementary at best, but I like to say that I’m embracing the slightly buggy aesthetic of useless early internet web pages!
