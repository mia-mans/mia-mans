Design Projects

As the lead Publicist for Penn Dance Company, I designed all company marketing including show promotions, new member recruitment, merchandise design, and more. Summit

Summit was Penn Dance Company’s National Geographic inspired production. While many of the thematic elements are drawn from the National Geographic brand, all layouts are entirely original. The photographs of dancers used throughout are from a photoshoot that I conceptualized and organized.

Auditions

In order to recruit new members each fall, Penn Dance Company creates content to be posted on social media, hung around the University of Pennsylvania’s campus, and presented at club fairs. I wanted to highlight the company's professionalism as well as its fun, social side.

New Member Introductions

Penn Dance welcomes each year’s new members with a photoshoot and series of Instagram introductions. This year, I created an Instagram reel inspired by professional dance companies’ new hire introductions as well as posts highlighting each individual. The posts and photoshoot were inspired by minimal streetwear magazines, appearing both professional and trendy.

Soirée

Soirée was Penn Dance Company’s Fall 2024 collaboration show with the Penn Glee Club, centered around a dinner party theme. The publicity was an elevated version of the trending dinner party aesthetic. From assembling the photoshoot setup to incorporating creative phrases throughout the copy, I ensured that all media was both functional and immersive.
