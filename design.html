Summit

Summit was Penn Dance Company’s National Geographic inspired production. While many of the thematic elements are drawn from the National Geographic brand, all layouts are entirely original. The photographs of dancers used throughout are from a photoshoot that I conceptualized and organized.

Auditions

In order to recruit new members each fall, Penn Dance Company creates content to be posted on social media, hung around the University of Pennsylvania’s campus, and presented at club fairs. I wanted to highlight the company's professionalism as well as our more fun, social side.
