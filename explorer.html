A social app created to encourage real life connections and getting out within your city.

Problem

While there are always exciting things to do and places to eat, many of us end up with decision paralysis when trying to decide what to do with friends and end up staying home instead.

This app encourages users to learn about events in their cities, plan itineraries with friends, and get involved in their communities. By combining features from platforms like Yelp, Apple Maps, and Pinterest, this app promotes collaborative exploration of your local neighborhoods.

Goals

• Allow users to easily browse establishments and events in an area within the current registration system

• Create an organized system to plan itineraries with others

• Provide users with a private space to save locations to their personal collections
